WORK PROCESS

Our Working Process

Client Assessment and Job Analysis

Understand the client's needs, preferences, and requirements. This involves conducting interviews, assessing resumes, and understanding the company culture and job specifications. Job consultants need to analyze job descriptions thoroughly to match candidates appropriately.

Candidate Sourcing and Screening

Utilize various channels such as job boards, social media platforms, and professional networks to source potential candidates. Once candidates are identified, job consultants screen them through interviews, skills assessments, and background checks to ensure they meet the job requirements and cultural fit.

Facilitating the Hiring Process

Act as intermediaries between clients and candidates, coordinating interviews, negotiating offers, and providing feedback. Job consultants also offer support to candidates throughout the hiring process, assisting with resume preparation, interview coaching, and salary negotiation. Additionally, they maintain relationships with both clients and candidates to foster long-term partnerships.
